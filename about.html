About Me

My name is Alberto Rivas, and I am currently pursuing a degree in Data Science and Engineering. I am passionate about solving complex problems with data and am constantly seeking opportunities to grow both technically and professionally.

In the upcoming academic year, I will be studying advanced courses in Engineering in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at La Sapienza University of Rome. This opportunity will allow me to deepen my knowledge in areas such as machine learning, AI systems, and software engineering—fields I am deeply interested in.

Recently, I completed a two-month internship at a leading pharmaceutical cooperative in Spain. During this experience, I worked with large Excel datasets from various pharmacy partners. My task was to clean and structure the data, extract key insights, and build a dynamic route prediction model. I applied machine learning techniques for demand forecasting, followed by clustering algorithms and the Whale Optimization Algorithm (WOA) to optimize delivery routes. The project not only improved my technical skills but also taught me how data can drive operational efficiency in the real world.

I consider myself a proactive and curious learner. I enjoy exploring new tools and methodologies, and I'm particularly drawn to the intersection between data analysis, AI, and decision-making. Whether it's a classroom project or an industry problem, I strive to approach each challenge with creativity, discipline, and a desire to make an impact.

My current technical toolkit includes Python, SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools like Tableau. I'm also expanding my skills in AI frameworks and optimization algorithms to better support data-driven decision-making.

If you're interested in collaboration, discussion, or simply sharing ideas—feel free to reach out!
